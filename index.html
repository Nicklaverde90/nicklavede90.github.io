I built a single platform where customers can search the catalog, customize parts with a guided configurator, get instant RFQs, and track orders/warranties.   • Download files like CAD and datasheets
  • Send performance feedback with photos + notes

Impact

  • Quoting became faster (from days to hours).
  • Reduced back-and-forth between sales and customers.
  • Customers can self-serve files 24/7.
